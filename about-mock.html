About

I am a 30+ fandom enjoyer with a love for many things audio — singing, turning my favorite fan fiction into podfics, and writing and voicing ASMR scripts inspired by fandom and more.

All original fan fics have been recorded with the permission or collaboration of the author.

My podfic work is a celebration of transforming inclusive, mostly queer fan-written works into accessible stories.

What's Podfic?

Podfics are the "audiobooks" of fan fiction.

Studies have shown fan fiction is a great outlet for identity exploration for queer folks. I aim to create a safe space for fans to fall even more in love with the stories and ships they enjoy.

I podfic my favourite stories out of pure enjoyment and the spirit of community — and I also take commissions for those who want me to read their work.

My general rule: if I enjoy the fic and feel I'm capable of voicing it, I'll record it.
